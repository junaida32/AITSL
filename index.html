About Us

AITSL is part of Pharaon Group also known as Attock group. AITSL is an emerging Information Technology services company. It is assisting the Attock Group Companies in adapting to and implementing Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition, SCADA, Plant Automation, Oil Movement & Storage Solution, Remote Well Head, Pipeline Management and Monitoring as well as other technology intensive solutions. AITSL has been catering to the demands of IT related outsourcing, centralization of IT operations and services, design and implementation of Local and Wide Area Networks, Wireless Connectivity, Supervising ERP Implementation and CCTV based security solutions. Attock Information Technology Services (Pvt.)
